About Fake Job Post Detecting

A modern solution to identify fraudulent job postings using advanced machine learning techniques.

What is Fake Job Predictor?

Fake Job Predictor is an AI-driven application that helps identify fraudulent job postings by analyzing job descriptions, company profiles, and other relevant information. It uses cutting-edge machine learning models to ensure job seekers can trust the postings they apply for.

Why Use Fake Job Predictor?

Protect Job Seekers

Identify scams and safeguard job seekers from fraudulent postings.

Advanced Analytics

Leverage machine learning models for accurate predictions.

Insights and Trends

Visualize job market insights with word clouds and analytics.

Quick and Efficient

Analyze and predict results with minimal effort.

How Does It Work?

The system uses a combination of text data from job titles, descriptions, company profiles, and other fields to train machine learning models. Users can upload datasets, preprocess the data, train the models, and use the prediction feature to analyze job postings in real-time.
